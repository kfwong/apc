ASEAN Philanthropy Dialogue with APC in 2018

As APC expands our Southeast Asian footprint, we recognise the importance to partner with and have an open dialogue with the governments in each country in order to achieve the maximum impact for our programmes. As Singapore will be hosting ASEAN in 2018, we have approached the Ministry of Foreign Affairs here to organise a dialogue with ASEAN ministers along the side lines of the main summit.

Members of APC, David Zuellig, Cherie Nursalim, Dr. Lee Oi Kum, and APC Chairman Stanley Tan, CEO Laurence Lien and Director Stacey Choe met with Minister of Foreign Affairs, Dr Vivian Balakrishnan, and his team on 17th August to discuss the strategy for the dialogue.

We are currently planning the event format and the programmes leading up to the summit. Please stay tuned as we will be writing to members with more detailed information and requests for your input and participation. We will be forming working groups on key sectors that APC members are involved in so that we will be able to invite and engage with the right corresponding agencies.
