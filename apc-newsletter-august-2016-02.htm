What China can learn from a platform like APC

APC HQ in Singapore hosted a delegation from China under the auspices of the upcoming China Charity Fair at our offices on 25th August. The 15-member delegation consisted of representatives from regional and national foundations and research agencies working on the new China Charity Law. Laurence and Stacey shared APC’s mission and activities, along with the Asian philanthropy landscape with the audience, who were very much

The dialogue was also informative for APC as we learnt more about the regulatory change in China with regards to establishing trusts. The delegates were abuzz with questions on how to mobilize philanthropists, understanding their motivations, and what China can and should learn from APC.

We look forward to hosting and partnering on such exchanges for Asian stakeholders to understand better on the trends and progress in the social sector across the region.
